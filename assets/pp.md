Columbia University's [History Lab](http://history-lab.org) and 
[MuckRock](https://muckrock.com) have assembled a database that 
already includes more than two hundred and twenty-five thousand
pages of records related to the government response to Covid-19
obtained through Freedom of Information Act requests by the 
Documenting COVID-19 project at 
[The Brown Institue for Media Innovation](https://brown.columbia.edu/).
